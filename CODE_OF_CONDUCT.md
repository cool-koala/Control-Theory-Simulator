# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, constructive collaboration.

- Be specific and professional in technical disagreement.
- Assume good intent, but be rigorous about behavior and evidence.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and dismissive language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or repeated bad-faith argument
- Publishing private information without permission
- Abusive or demeaning language

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate these expectations.
